Anywhere - Anytime

Refy is an accredited online school that provides a premier online learning experience that honors each child’s individual learning style and educational needs. Refy offers a challenging course of study for students in sixth through twelth grades, with college prep academics, and Honors opportunities. Our philosophy of education respects each child’s gifts, talents, and learning style. At Refy, students enjoy personalized, one-to-one teaching and learning that is not often found in a home school setting.

Refy offers quality high school, college prep, honors academic programs and curriculum options for middle school as well. Our interactive online and common core aligned text-based courses inspire critical thinking, and are supported by caring teachers who will build one-on-one relationships with each child for a positive learning experience. Families that choose homeschooling will find our rich and varied curriculum a valuable resource for their needs. We use the Blackboard Learning Management System (LMS), which is the world’s number one platform for interactive learning. Within our LMS you will be able to build a portfolio for post-secondary applications and communicate with others in your courses through the Discussion Board feature.

Mission

Refy is committed to providing interactive, media-rich, college preparatory, and educational opportunities for grades 6-12 in a flexible, student-centered learning environment to a diverse population of learners.
